Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy describes how Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta LLC ("Company", "we" or "our") collects, uses, stores and protects the personal information provided by you ("User" or "you") when using the Our site. By using the site, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. This online privacy policy (this “Policy”) applies only to information collected through websites owned by Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta LLC. 1.1. Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide, such as your name, email address, telephone number, postal address and payment information, when you fill out forms on our website, place a service request or contact us.

1.2. Usage Information: We collect information about your use of the website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on the website, and other similar tracking information. This information is collected automatically through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2.1. By using this Site, you consent to the collection, use, disclosure and transfer of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not consent to the collection, use, disclosure and transfer of your information as described in this Policy, you may not use this Site.

3.1. We use the information collected for the following purposes:

3.1.1. Provide and improve our services, including processing requests, scheduling cleaning services, and providing customer support.

3.1.2. Personalize the user experience and deliver relevant content based on your preferences.

3.1.3. Send information about our services, promotional offers, updates or related communications.

3.1.4. Conduct internal analysis and research to improve our services and understand user needs and preferences.

3.1.5. Ensure the security and integrity of the website and protect our legal rights.

3.1.6. Recruit new collaborators or employees, include run background checks.

4.1. We do not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy or where we obtain your prior consent.

4.6. We may also disclose your information to prevent or investigate possible crime, such as fraud or identity theft; to protect the security of this Site; to enforce or reinforce our online Terms of Use or other agreements; or to protect our own rights or property or the rights, property or safety of our users or others.

5.1. We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your use of the website. By continuing to use the site, you consent to the use of cookies and similar tracking technologies.

5.3. Some third parties may serve you cookies for a variety of reasons. For example, we use Google Analytics, a web analytics tool that helps us understand how visitors interact with our Sites. To learn more about Google Analytics, read the Google Analytics definition here.
